Meet Yuri

Thank you for visiting my page. I have lived in Kanazawa for over 23 years, and I truly love this city.

My Journey

Over the years, I have watched Kanazawa change and grow. I enjoy discovering small details that many visitors might miss.

Experience Abroad

I spent one year in Sydney, Australia. Living abroad helped me understand what it feels like to visit a new country for the first time.

Why I Guide

I am not just a guide who shows famous places. I want to gently connect the people who live in Kanazawa with the people who visit. I hope to share not only the sights, but also the daily life and warmth of this city. I want our time together to become a special memory for you.

Credentials

  • 23 years living in Kanazawa
  • 1 year in Sydney
  • TOEIC L&R 875
  • RSA (NSW)
  • Certificate 3 in Health Service Assistance

Let’s Make Your Time in Kanazawa Memorable

If my way of guiding feels right for you, I would be happy to show you around Kanazawa.
